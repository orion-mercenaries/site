Introduction

Turning Devona into a spirit spammer is great for presearing.

We use our Spirit Spammer guide as a base. But in presearing we won't have access to Vampirism and Summon Spirits, because these skills are not unlocked account wide.

Instead we replace those skills with:

  • Flesh of My Flesh
  • Splinter Weapon
