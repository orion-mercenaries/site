Introduction

This is a build for players with brand new accounts. It is meant to be played in the Nightfall campaign. The original build can be found here.

Preparation

You have to ascend in order to be able to change your secondary profession, which is required to purchase skills for heroes not matching your initial profession combination.

Unlocking Heroes

Nightfall Heroes

  • Dunkoro, Tahlkora, Melonni: Follow the primary quests of the Nightfall campaign to unlock these heroes.
  • Acolyte Sousuke: Follow the primary quests until you have completed Bad Tide Rising. Talk to Field General Hayao in Sun Docks and accept the quest Zaishen Elite. Complete it, then choose the quest Student Sousuke afterwards. Upon completion, Acolyte Sousuke will be unlocked.
  • Olias: Follow the primary quests until you can access Consulate Docks. Talk to Dinja and accept the quest All for One and One for Justice. Upon completion, Olias will be unlocked.

Eye of the North Heroes

  • Vekk (and Ogden): Talk to Bendah in Kamadan and accept the quest Hole of Istan. Upon reaching Boreal Station, Vekk (and Ogden) will be unlocked.
  • Gwen: Follow the primary quest Against the Destroyers and travel to the Eye of the North. If you are not max level, use the level 20 henchmen in Boreal Station as help. Upon watching The First Vision for the first time, Gwen will be unlocked.
