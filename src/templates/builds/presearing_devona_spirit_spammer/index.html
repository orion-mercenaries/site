Introduction

Turning Devona into a spirit spammer is great for presearing. The skills can also be easily unlocked on a new account.

To unlock the skills on a new account, start a new Factions Ritualist character and proceed with the campaign until you get to Kaineng Center. (Once you reach The Marketplace you can simply follow the western coastline north across Bukdek Byway.)

Once in Kaineng Center, go to the Skill Trainer and purchace the following skills (remember to save skill points for this while leveling):

  • Painful Bond
  • Pain
  • Shadow Song
  • BloodSong
  • Armor of Unfeeling
  • Spirit Siphon
  • Union
  • Flesh of My Flesh
